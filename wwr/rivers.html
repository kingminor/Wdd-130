Rivers

Green River (Section A, Flaming Gorge)

Difficulty: Class I-II (Beginner)

This section is known for its scenic cliffs and calm waters, making it ideal for beginners and families looking for a relaxed rafting experience.

Snake River (Alpine Canyon)

Difficulty: Class II-III (Moderate)

Located near Jackson Hole, this river provides thrilling rapids amidst scenic views, ideal for intermediate rafters.

Payette River

Difficulty: Main Fork - Class III (Moderate)
South Fork - Class IV (Difficult)

The Main Fork offers a family-friendly adventure, while the South Fork presents exciting challenges for advanced rafters.

Salmon River

Difficulty: Class III-IV (Moderate to Difficult)

Known as the "River of No Return," it offers both calm stretches and thrilling rapids, with plenty of scenic wilderness.

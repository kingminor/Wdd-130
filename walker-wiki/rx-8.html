The RX-8 is an advanced drone developed by the tech company Red Nexus during the Dark Era. Designed primarily for surveillance and control, the RX-8 is a militarized version of the ancient AV8 drone. The RX-8 drones are feared by civilians due to their role in maintaining order through intimidation and force. These drones play a crucial part in Red Nexus's strategy to dominate and control major cities, making them a symbol of the oppressive and authoritarian rule that characterizes the Dark Era.

Summmary

Red Nexus is a pivotal entity in the World of Walker universe, introduced during the Modern Era of the storyline. Initially appearing as a philanthropic and innovative tech company, Red Nexus specializes in DNA research, life enhancement, security systems, and weapons development. The organization funds key archaeological research into the lost ancient civilization of the Walkers, enabling critical discoveries such as the AV8 drone and prophecy discs. However, their true motives reveal a darker side as they exploit these findings for control and domination.

During the Dark Era, Red Nexus evolves into a totalitarian regime, controlling major cities and enforcing their rule with militarized RX8 drones, a feared weapon that suppresses civilians. Their actions catalyze the formation of the Unity Force, a resistance group led by Walkers, who wage a 40-year drone war against Red Nexus. Despite their initial rise to power, Red Nexus is ultimately overthrown in a grueling conflict, leaving their legacy as a cautionary tale of unchecked ambition and technological misuse in the World of Walker.

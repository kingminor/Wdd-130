Summmary

The Spectre, a key artifact in the World of Walker universe, is an advanced technological tool created by the ancient Walkers. Hidden within a prominent monument featured in multiple music videos, the Spectre enabled the ancient civilization to foresee future catastrophic events, such as a devastating solar storm and their own extinction due to a meteor shower. To preserve their knowledge and protect future generations, the Walkers buried time capsules containing prophecy discs and instructions for survival. This foresight and the Spectre's role symbolize the Walkers' commitment to safeguarding humanity's future despite their demise.

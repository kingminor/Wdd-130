Summmary

The AVI-8, also known as the AV-8 drone, is a pivotal piece of technology in the World of Walker universe. Originally developed by the ancient Walkers, the AV8 drone is renowned for its advanced capabilities. It features dual lenses: one for scanning and another for constructing, making it an invaluable tool for both exploration and building. The drone is operated through hand movements by its pilot, allowing for precise control. When not in use, it can be compacted into a small, metal Walker logo, but when deployed, it expands into a full-sized drone. The AV8 plays a crucial role in the Walkers' efforts to survive and rebuild after the solar storm, as it is used for mass production and strategic operations. Its versatility and advanced technology make it a symbol of the Walkers' ingenuity and resilience.

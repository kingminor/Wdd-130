Overview

Purpose

I want to make the story in the Alan Walker Cinematic Universe more accessible to new Audiances. This will be done by providing clear and easy to read timelines about the various parts of the universe, as well as individual wiki pages on major topics.

Audience

There are two main audiances, people who already know about the Alan Walker Cinematic Universe and people who don't. This will primarily be people who enjoy Science Fiction and Fantasy Stories. There will likely be a group of technical people who use this website.
